Smartphone Accelerator Data Dictionary
========================================================

### Human/Activity features

* __SubjectId__ Volunteer id for the experiments (1-30)
* __ActivityLabel__ Activity label one of: 
    - WALKING
    - WALKING_UPSTAIRS
    - WALKING_DOWNSTAIRS
    - SITTING
    - STANDING
    - LAYING
